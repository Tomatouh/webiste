🖥 PC Build Assistance

🔍

Service Purpose

Building a PC isn't just about putting parts together – it means optimization, compatibility, efficiency, and aesthetics. At InfoDam Solutions, we offer full support to build your ideal system, whether you're a gamer, video editor, programmer, or regular user.

🧠

Initial Consultation

  • We discuss your actual needs: gaming, productivity, multitasking, silence, etc.
  • We establish your desired resolution and refresh rate (e.g.: 1080p at 144Hz, 1440p at 165Hz, 4K at 60Hz, etc.)
  • We identify the ideal platform per case: Intel/AMD & NVIDIA/AMD Radeon/Intel ARC
  • We determine the required PSU wattage, focusing on efficiency
🧩

Component Selection

  • Graphics cards based on target FPS/resolution
  • Balanced CPUs according to system needs
  • Optimal RAM (minimum DDR4/DDR5 with competitive frequencies)
  • Proper storage solutions (NVMe SSD/SATA SSD/HDD)
  • Case suitable for airflow and aesthetics
  • Appropriate cooling: air or All-in-One water cooling
🧱

Build and Assembly

  • Professional mounting of all components, with maximum attention
  • Clean cable management, routing through the back of the case using ties, Velcro straps, and dedicated guides
  • Mounting RGB controllers, extra fans, sound insulation pads (if needed)
  • Installing the OS, drivers, BIOS updates, and necessary utilities
📈

Testing and Optimization

  • Stress tests (CPU, GPU, memory, temperatures)
  • Benchmarks for relevant games or applications
  • Tuning for silence or performance (e.g.: fan profiles, undervolt, XMP enabled)
  • Monitor compatibility check (e.g.: G-Sync/FreeSync support, Hz/resolution settings)
📍
