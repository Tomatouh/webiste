🧰 Hardware Maintenance

🔍

What's included in hardware maintenance?

  1. 🧽 Full cleaning
    • Controlled disassembly of the system (desktop/laptop)
    • Dust removal from case, fans, heatsink, power supply
    • Cleaning ports (USB, HDMI, audio jack) and exposed components
    • Fine vacuuming + professional compressed air
❄️

Cooling system improvement

  • Replacing thermal paste for CPU and GPU
  • Checking/alignment of heatsinks, mounts, thermal pads
  • Temperature testing at idle/load before and after service
  • Fan upgrade/recommendation (if needed)
🔌

Component check

  • Testing RAM, SSD/HDD
  • Power supply voltages, power stability
  • Check for oxidized contacts, loose cables, motherboard sockets
  • Detect potential hardware issues before they become critical
🖥️

General system overhaul

  • Physical integrity check of motherboard, PCIe, RAM slots
  • Cable management for better airflow
  • Dust filter cleaning and fan adjustment
  • Upgrade recommendations if applicable
